Valentine's Day

Valentine's Day is a holiday celebrated on February 14th, mainly in Western countries. It is a day dedicated to celebrating love and affection between intimate companions. People typically express their love for each other by giving gifts, flowers, or chocolates and sharing romantic gestures such as candlelight dinners or handwritten love letters.
